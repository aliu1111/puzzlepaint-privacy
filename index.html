Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Solo Gaming Studios handles user information in its mobile application (the “App”), which includes coloring and puzzle features.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal information about users.

However, the App may use third-party advertising services (such as Google AdMob) that collect and use a device’s Advertising ID for the purpose of showing personalized or non-personalized ads.

The Advertising ID is a non-permanent, anonymous identifier provided by your device’s operating system and can be reset at any time in your device settings.

2. Use of the Advertising ID

  • To display ads relevant to user interests.
  • To measure ad performance and improve ad quality.
  • To limit the frequency of repeated ads.

No personally identifiable information is collected or linked to this ID by Solo Gaming Studios.

3. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob – for serving advertisements.

These third-party partners may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. For more information, please visit Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since only anonymous advertising IDs are used, no personal information from children is stored or shared.

5. Your Choices

You can reset or disable your Advertising ID at any time in your device’s settings:

  • On Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset Advertising ID
  • On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → Allow Apps to Request to Track

6. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the App or on this page with the revised effective date.
